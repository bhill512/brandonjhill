About

I’m a Colorado Springs-based remote software engineer with an operator mindset. I focus on disciplined execution, clear communication, and ownership—building systems that are easy to run, maintain, and trust.

I’m a US Army infantry veteran with deployments to Iraq and Afghanistan. That experience shaped how I work: steady under pressure, process-aware, and committed to doing the basics well.

I earned both a BS and MS in Computer Science from Colorado Technical University, and I spend most of my time shipping practical tools, integrating systems, and improving reliability in .NET and TypeScript/JavaScript.
